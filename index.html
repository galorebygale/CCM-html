About CCM

Câmara de Comércio de Moçambique, CCM is an association of non-profit businesses and businesses
incorporated under Mozambican law, that are autonomous in terms of their administration, finances
and assets.
CCM is dedicated to promoting the harmonious development of its members' businesses with a view
to ensuring effective participation in the country's economic activities.
